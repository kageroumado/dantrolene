# Dantrolene Privacy Policy

*Last updated: July 13, 2026 · canonical version: [kagerou.glass/dantrolene/privacy](https://kagerou.glass/dantrolene/privacy/)*

Dantrolene does not collect, store, transmit, or share any data. Period.

## Location access

macOS requires Location Services permission for any app that reads the name (SSID) of the WiFi network you are connected to. Dantrolene requests this permission for exactly one purpose: comparing the current network name against the home network you designated, entirely on your Mac. Your location, network names, and any other information never leave your device.

## What Dantrolene does not do

- No analytics or telemetry
- No crash reporting
- No advertising or tracking identifiers
- No third-party SDKs
- No network connections of any kind — the app has no server to talk to
- No accounts, no sign-in

## Data stored locally

Your settings (chosen mode, home network name, display sleep timeout) are stored in the app's standard preferences on your Mac and can be removed by deleting the app.
